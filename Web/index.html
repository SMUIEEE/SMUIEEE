Southern Methodist University IEEE Student Chapter

Stronger Engineers are here to help you make connections, find support, and guide your journey...

The SMU IEEE Student Chapter is a dynamic hub for innovation and technology enthusiasts at Southern Methodist University. As part of the global IEEE organization, the chapter connects Electrical and Computer Engineering students, as well as anyone passionate about technological advancements, to invaluable resources, industry leaders, and hands-on learning experiences. With a focus on fostering creativity and technical growth, the chapter offers three main avenues for engagement: the Design & Innovation Club for collaborative projects, inspiring guest lectures from professionals, and practical tutorials to build technical skills. Join us to ignite your potential and drive the future of technology!
